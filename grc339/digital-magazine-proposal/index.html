Introduction

The Lineup

Coachella has a set list. We cover the other one. The Lineup is the only publication dedicated to the food and drink culture of the world's most iconic music festival. Every vendor, every collab, every delicious bite, we're on it. From the hidden gem tucked behind the Sahara tent to the celebrity chef pop-up everyone's talking about, The Lineup treats Coachella's food scene with the same obsession that fans bring to the music. Two weekends a year. Endless flavor. This is your guide.
